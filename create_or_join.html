    1. In each round, players will be given a scale of 1 to 10, with descriptive words representing the extremes (e.g., Ancient to Modern).

    3. Other players vote on what number they think matches the clue. Points are awarded as follows:

      • 1 point for being 1 off in either direction.
    4. Each player gets two turns as the clue giver. Once everyone has had their turns, the player with the most points wins!
