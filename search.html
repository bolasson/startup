Title: The Alamo

Gameplay: One player is selected to go play as the attacker, while all of the other players gather in a fort on the other side of the game field as defenders. The defenders may not leave the fort. When a defender is hit, they leave the fort and become an attacker. When an attacker is hit they must touch the wall on the opposite side of the fort. They respawn and keep attacking. The defenders win if they hold the fort for 7 minutes.

Rating: 5/5 Stars

Tags: 'Large Area' 'Large Group' 'Small Group' 'High Rating'
